Our Story

It all started with a simple chat, a few exchanged emojis, and small talk between classes. Neither of us thought much of it at first—but one message turned into daily conversations, silly jokes, and slowly, something deeper.

And now, after all those years and memories, we’re finally here—ready to start our forever. 💙
